Privacy Policy

Revline Performance Garage respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Your vehicle information
  • Photos or videos of your vehicle

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Communicate with you regarding appointments and services
  • Process payments and provide invoices
  • Improve our services and customer experience

Information Sharing

Your information will never be sold to third parties. It may only be disclosed if required by law or with your explicit consent.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Media Consent

By engaging our services, you agree that Revline Performance Garage may photograph or video your vehicle. These materials may be shared on our website, social media, and marketing channels. If you prefer not to have your vehicle featured, please inform us in writing prior to your appointment.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data. Please contact us for assistance.
